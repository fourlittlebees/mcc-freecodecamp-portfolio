Romance Author Beverly Jenkins

  • 1951: Born in Detroit, MI
  • 1969: Began college at Michigan State University
  • 1994: Published her first romance novel, Night Song
  • 2013: NAACP Image Award for Literature Nominee
  • 2017: Received the RWA Nora Roberts Lifetime Achievement Award
